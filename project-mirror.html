Mira Smart Mirror

An intelligent mirror system featuring automated defogging, ambient data display, and smart home integration.

Fall 2025
Role: Power & Hardware
ESP32
C++

The Challenge

After a hot shower, the most annoying thing is a foggy mirror that renders itself useless.

To solve this, my team and I designed Mira: a mirror that actively prevents fogging through intelligent hot air circulation and automated wipers. Beyond utility, it serves as an information hub, displaying time, weather, and date to start your day informed.

My Contributions

I led the Power Subsystem Design and hardware integration. My focus was ensuring the MCU and high-load components (fans/motors) operated safely together.

  • Designed schematics for the ESP32 MCU, 12V power distribution, DHT22 sensors, and LCD display.
  • Executed precise soldering of peripherals and heating fans to the main control board.
  • Implemented relay logic to control the 12V Stepper Motors and high-power fans via code.

Technical Implementation

From rough schematics to the final PCB layout. The system handles 12V for mechanical parts and 5V/3.3V for logic.
