ECG signal capturing and processing on MATLAB.

Briefly

Three opamps that capture that ECG signal from the body and filter it out through three different filters including low pass and high pass filter, then processing these signals on MATLAB digitally.

My part

I designed ECG signal capturing circuit as well as processing the signal on MATLAB.

  • Designed active op-amps filters including low pass and high pass filters to collect ECG signal through electrode robes. The lowpass filter was used to filter out the DC component of the signal while the high pass filter was used to filter high unwanted signals.
  • Collected and processed the digital ECG signal in MatLab through a digital low-pass filter to reduce the noise in the ECG signal. Removed the 60 Hz interference by modifying the Fourier coefficients.
