ECG Monitoring System

ECG signal capturing via analog circuits and digital signal processing on MATLAB.

Fall 2025
Analog Design
MATLAB

Overview

Electrocardiogram (ECG) monitoring is critical for detecting heart irregularities. This project involved building the entire signal chain from scratch: capturing the raw bio-potential signal from the body using electrodes, filtering it through analog circuits, and finally digitizing and cleaning it using software algorithms.

Technical Implementation

I designed the analog front-end circuit to capture the millivolt-level signals and the digital back-end to make them readable.

  • Analog Filtering: Designed active op-amp circuits including a high-pass filter (to remove DC offset/body movement) and a low-pass filter (to remove high-frequency noise).
  • Amplification: Implemented an instrumentation amplifier configuration to boost the weak heart signal to a usable voltage level.
  • Digital Signal Processing: Imported the raw data into MATLAB. I applied a digital notch filter to specifically remove the 60 Hz power line interference using Fourier analysis.
